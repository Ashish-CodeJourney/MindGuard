Reclaim your attention. MindGuard is a privacy-first Android app that automatically detects and blocks short-form video feeds — Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Snapchat Spotlight — entirely on-device.

FEATURES
• Reel / Shorts / Spotlight blocking for Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and Snapchat
• Per-app toggles to enable or disable blocking per app
• Focus schedule with configurable hours (supports overnight ranges)
• Pause focus mode for 30 minutes from the app or notification shade
• Quick Settings tile for tap-to-toggle without opening the app
• Daily and all-time statistics (blocks, attempts, streaks)
• 100% on-device — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking

HOW IT WORKS
MindGuard uses Android's Accessibility Service to inspect UI events only from the four target apps. When the short-video player is detected on screen, the service navigates away automatically before you can get drawn in.

PRIVACY
• All detection runs entirely on-device
• No network requests
• No user accounts or sign-in
• Service scope restricted to Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and Snapchat
• No data is collected, stored remotely, or transmitted

REQUIREMENTS
• Android 7.0+ (API 24+)
• Accessibility Service permission (granted manually in system settings)
